About Our Company

Spandan Robotics is an AI and robotics company dedicated to create socially intelligent machines that boosts the quality of our lives.


Established by Spandan Manna, Spandan Robotics Pvt. Ltd, is an ISO 9001 - 2008 certified company. The company was formed in the year 2021 at Kalimpong, W.B, India. The Spandan Team has built a global reputation for creating robots that look and act genuinely alive. Our innovations in AI research and development, robotics engineering, experimental design, storytelling and material science bring robots to life as engaging characters, useful products, and as devoloping AI.
